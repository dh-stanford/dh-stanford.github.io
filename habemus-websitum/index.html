Digital Humanities @ Stanford

Habemus websitum!

Submitted By: Glen Worthey on Wed Aug 14 2013 10:11:37 GMT-0700 (Pacific Daylight Time)

We Stanford Digital Humanists (a.k.a. DHers), scattered as we are across campus, have long talked about uniting under the banner of a collective website.  Now we have: here it is, and here we are.

We'll highlight some of the projects we're involved in, whether completed or in progress; we'll introduce ourselves to, our interests, our activities, and our evolving organizational structure.  We use this site to announce workshops (like our upcoming  series for the Stanford community) and other DH activities, whether at Stanford, in the San Francisco Bay Area, or in the larger DH community.
